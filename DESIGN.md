## Overview

A "Linear-inspired" dark workspace for legal professionals. The aesthetic combines the precision of high-end SaaS (near-black neutrals, tight spacing) with the personality of "vintage etching" and organic elements like the Naya fern.

## Branded Elements
- **Vintage Etching:** High-contrast, dithered-style visual elements.
- **Organic Texture:** Constant use of the fern imagery to ground the high-tech UI.
- **Golden Ratio Type:** Scale is strictly derived from 1.618 (φ) starting at 16px (1rem).

## Do's and Don'ts
- **DO:** Use `Fansan Display` for all major headings.
- **DO:** Stick to the 8px base grid for all padding and margins.
- **DON'T:** Use system default fonts for primary display text.
- **DON'T:** Use generic purple/violet gradients; stick to the lime accent (#b1e244).
